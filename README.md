## The BDIX Advantage — Why It Matters

Okay, quick explainer for anyone who isn't familiar: BDIX stands for Bangladesh Internet Exchange. When a hosting server is connected to BDIX, data transfer between that server and users inside Bangladesh doesn't have to go through international gateways. The result? Much faster page load times for local visitors.

If you're building a site that's primarily for a Bangladeshi audience — a school portal, a local news site, an e-commerce store targeting Dhaka customers — BDIX hosting genuinely makes a difference. You can feel it when navigating the site.

Hostever was one of the early providers to offer BDIX-connected hosting at budget prices, and it's still one of their main selling points.
